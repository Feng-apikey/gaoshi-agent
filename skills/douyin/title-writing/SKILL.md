# Title Writing (标题撰写)

## Overview

Title writing for Douyin is about creating thumb-stopping headlines that compete with video content in a high-speed feed. Titles must be concise, punchy, and optimized for Douyin's fast-paced consumption style.

## Douyin Title Limits

| Content Type | Title Limit |
|---|---|
| Video | ≤55 characters |
| Image-Text | ≤55 characters |
| Article | ≤30 characters |

## Core Frameworks

### 1. Number + Hook
"5个让你效率翻倍的技巧"
"这3个习惯改变了我的一年"

### 2. Curiosity Gap
"没想到这个方法这么好用"
"99%的人不知道的隐藏功能"

### 3. Pain Point + Solution
"学不会XX? 试试这个"
"还在为XX发愁? 看这里"

### 4. Result Promise
"坚持30天,变化惊呆了"
"用了这个方法,我再也没XX"

### 5. Bold Claim
"这是我用过最好的XX替代品"
"比XX好用100倍"

## Power Words

- **Urgency**: 必看、立即、现在、最新
- **Numbers**: 5个、3步、7天、30分钟
- **Results**: 惊呆、逆袭、爆火、翻倍
- **Curiosity**: 秘密、没想到、居然、原来

## Douyin-Specific Tips

- Front-load important words — only first 15-20 chars show in feed
- Avoid clickbait that doesn't deliver — high bounce rate hurts algorithm
- Oral/conversational tone (口语化) performs better than formal
- Use emoji sparingly — 1-2 max, not a wall of emoji
- #话题标签 don't count toward title characters
- Tags should be relevant, not oversized (account weight limits big tag visibility)

## Implementation

1. Write 5-10 variant titles
2. Pick the one that creates strongest curiosity
3. Front-load the hook
4. Test: would YOU stop scrolling to click this?
5. Track CTR across posts, iterate on winners
