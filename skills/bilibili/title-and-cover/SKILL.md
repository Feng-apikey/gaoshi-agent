# Title and Cover Design (标题与封面)

## Overview

On Bilibili, the title + cover combination is the single biggest factor in click-through rate (CTR). Unlike feed-based platforms where content auto-plays, B站 users actively choose which video to click. The cover must communicate value at a glance; the title must create enough curiosity or promise enough value to earn the click.

## Title Guidelines

### Limits
- **Title**: ≤80 characters
- First 30 characters most visible in recommendation feed
- Avoid special characters that break on mobile

### Effective Title Patterns

**1. Value + Specificity**
"从零到精通: Python数据分析完整教程(附代码)"
"7天带你搭建自己的AI助手 | 完整项目实战"

**2. Controversial/Discussion**
"为什么我觉得XX被严重高估了"
"用了3年,我终于放弃XX了"

**3. Tutorial/How-To**
"手把手教你XX | 新手也能学会"
"别再XX了,试试这个方法"

**4. Comparison**
"XX vs YY: 到底哪个更适合你?"
"100元 vs 1000元的XX,差别有多大?"

**5. List/Collection**
"5个改变我编程习惯的神器"
"今年最值得看的10本书(附详细解读)"

### Title Writing Process

1. Identify core value: what does viewer learn/get?
2. Brainstorm 10+ variant titles
3. Filter: which title would make YOU click?
4. Front-load key info: first 30 chars visible in feed
5. Test: track CTR across videos, iterate

## Cover Design

### B站 Cover Specs
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 horizontal
- **Resolution**: 1920×1080 minimum
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 5MB

### Cover Elements

1. **Face (highly recommended)**: Human faces with strong expressions drive 2-3x higher CTR
2. **Text overlay**: 3-8 characters max — bold, high contrast, readable at small size
3. **Visual style**: Clean, not cluttered. One focal point.
4. **Branding**: Consistent style across all covers creates recognition

### Cover Design Principles

**The 3-Second Test**: Can someone understand what the video is about from the cover in 3 seconds?

```
GOOD COVER:                    BAD COVER:
- One clear subject            - Cluttered, too many elements
- 3-8 character text           - Long text, unreadable thumbnail
- High contrast, bold colors   - Low contrast, washed out
- Expressive face/reaction     - Generic screenshot
- Consistent branding          - Random style each video
```

### Text on Covers

**Do**:
- 3-8 characters max
- Bold font, white or bright color
- Dark outline/shadow for contrast
- Top or bottom third placement
- Key message only

**Don't**:
- Full title on cover (redundant, unreadable)
- Small text (invisible in thumbnail)
- Low contrast text on busy background

## Title + Cover Synergy

The title and cover must work together:
- Cover shows the emotion/result
- Title explains the value/specifics
- Together they tell a complete story

Example:
- Cover: Before/after comparison image + "30天变化"
- Title: "坚持30天每天XX,我的变化惊呆了所有人"

## Implementation

1. Design cover FIRST (establishes emotional hook)
2. Write title SECOND (adds specific value)
3. Review together: do they make someone want to click?
4. A/B test: try different cover styles for similar content
5. Track CTR in B站 analytics, iterate

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Title + cover say same thing | They should complement, not repeat |
| Cover text too long | 3-8 characters max |
| No face on cover | Faces boost CTR 2-3x |
| Clickbait with no substance | Deliver on title's promise |
